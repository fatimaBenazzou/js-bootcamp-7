Why AURA X1?

Engineered for the details that matter.

🎵 Hi-Res Audio

40mm custom-tuned drivers deliver a frequency response of 4Hz–40kHz — far beyond what standard headphones can reproduce. Every instrument, every breath, every detail.

🔇 Active Noise Cancellation

Dual-microphone ANC technology filters up to 98% of ambient noise, letting you focus entirely on your music — or your silence. Three modes: Full ANC, Transparency, Off.

🔋 40-Hour Battery Life

A single charge powers up to 40 hours of playback with ANC on. Quick-charge support gives you 5 hours of listening from just 10 minutes plugged in via USB-C.

🎙️ Call Clarity

Six-microphone array with beamforming technology isolates your voice from background noise for crystal-clear calls, every time — whether you're on a busy street or in a noisy café.

☁️ All-Day Comfort

Memory foam ear cushions and an ultra-lightweight titanium headband make AURA X1 comfortable for hours of continuous wear. At just 248g, you'll forget you're wearing them.

📱 Seamless Connectivity

Bluetooth 5.3 with multipoint connection — stay paired to your phone and laptop simultaneously with zero switching friction. Works wired too via the included 3.5mm cable.
